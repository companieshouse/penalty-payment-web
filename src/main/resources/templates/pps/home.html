Pay a penalty to Companies House

Use this service to make an online payment by credit or debit card if you've received a penalty notice from Companies House.

You'll need:

  • to sign in to or create a Companies House account (this is different to a WebFiling account)
  • a credit or debit card
  • the information on your penalty notice (that we sent you in the post)
I've lost my penalty notice

Email enquiries@companieshouse.gov.uk to let us know if you have lost your penalty notice. Use 'Lost penalty notice' as the email subject.

Tell us as much information as you can about the penalty so we can pass your message to the right team.

You must pay the full amount.

It takes around 5 minutes to pay.

I want to appeal a penalty

If you have a late filing penalty

This option is only for penalties for late filing of accounts. You can appeal a late filing penalty (for filing accounts late) through Companies House. You must show that the reasons are exceptional.

If you have any other type of penalty

You can only appeal through the courts. Read guidance from Advicenow or contact a solicitor for help with this.

You have 28 days from when the penalty was issued to make an appeal. If you've submitted an appeal to the courts, you need to tell us by email or in writing (within 7 days).

You cannot appeal directly to Companies House, and we are not able to offer legal advice.
